Welcome to the Lesotho Tourism Expo

The Lesotho Tourism Expo is an event that showcases the beauty, culture, and adventure opportunities available in the Mountain Kingdom. The expo also creates opportunities for tourism businesses, investors and international visitors to discover the potential of Lesotho as a travel destination.

Why Visit Lesotho?

Lesotho offers one of the most unique tourism experiences in Southern Africa. Known as the "Kingdom in the Sky", the country is famous for its dramatic mountain scenery, high altitude landscapes and rich cultural heritage. Visitors can enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, pony trekking and mountain biking while also learning about the traditional Basotho way of life.

Tourism events such as the Lesotho Tourism Expo help promote the country as a destination for adventure tourism, cultural tourism and eco-tourism. Through exhibitions, workshops and live demonstrations, visitors gain insight into the tourism industry and the opportunities available for investment and travel.
